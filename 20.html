ISDS - Ingress Shareable Deploy Scheme
R*[@D][$a]R[@D][$a]R[@D][$a]R[@D][$a]R[@D][$a]R[@D][$a]R[@D][$a]R[@D][$a] [M1 + M2 [+ M3 + M4] | + | ++ ]
  • R* Stands for the northmost Resonator, indicated by a red dot in the scanner
  • R stands for resonators, those are written as deployed clockwise with the northmost resonator first
  • @D is an optional (as indicated by []) description of Distance from portal as a number 0-9 where 0 is on top of portal and 9 is as far as possible from the portal wich applies to all resonators left to the @D until another @D is on the left of it
  • $a is an optional value that applies in the same way as @D, to the resonators left to it in the series that indicates wich agent (a-z) that will place that specific resonator
  • M1, M2, M3 & M4 is Mods from left to right, if only one agent is involved M3 and M4 is optional. no mod is Indicated by a space (" "). No mods at all are indicated with a trailing + after the resonator sequence ("00000000+") for one agent and ++ for multiple agents ("00000000++")
Note that all 8 resonator slots has to be written out, an empty slot is indicated with a 0

Examples

8766504@94@0 VRS + LA + + (Image tbd) A R8 on the northmost position, right to it a R7, Right to it a R6, right to it a R6, right to it a R5, Right to it no Resonator, right to it a R4 all those are placed as far as the action radius allow from the portal, right to it another R4 placed directly at the portal. a very rare shield deployed in the first mod slot and a link amp in the second mod slot. Two or more agents involved. 87665543 (Image tbd) A R8 at the northmost position, right to it, in order a R7, two R6, two R5, one R4 and one R3. No mods/mods doesn't matter. Distance doesn't matter. One or more agents. 87665543+ (Image tbd) A R8 at the northmost position, right to it, in order a R7, two R6, two R5, one R4 and one R3. No mods. Distance doesn't matter. One agent.
This specification is aimed to give Ops a better overlook of how their field agents have deployed the involved portals, or to be able to give simple instructions to agents on field how to deploy the resonators and mods.
